Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does your agency do? +
We are a full-service e-commerce agency. We handle everything from finding profitable products to sell, setting up your store on Shopify, eBay, or Amazon, managing your inventory, and running ads to bring in customers.
Do I need to hold my own inventory? +
Not necessarily! We specialize in Dropshipping and Amazon FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon). This means suppliers or Amazon warehouses can handle the packing and shipping directly to your customers.
Do I need an existing store to work with you? +
No, you don't. We can build a brand new, highly optimized store for you from scratch. If you already have a store that isn't making enough sales, we can audit and improve it.
How long does it take to see results? +
While every store is different, our clients typically start seeing an increase in consistent traffic and sales within the first 30 to 60 days of implementing our tailored strategies.
